About


I am a Ph.D. candidate at McGill University, School of Information Studies since 2014. I am fortunate to be supervised by Dr. Benjamin C. M. Fung and work with him as well as other excellent colleagues in the Data Mining and Security Lab (DMaS). My research interests lie in discovering interesting patterns and knowledge in very large amounts of data, and utilizing this knowledge to resolve real-life application problems in cyber investigation, literacy education, information management and semantic web development, etc.
